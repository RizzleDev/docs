A guide on how to buy & enable premium

## Buying and enabling your Premium subscription

Buying a Premium subscription has never been easier!

Go to the premium page

Go to https://Birbdbot.dev/premium

2

/

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the premium page">
     https://Birbdbot.dev/premium
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Select the plan thats right for you, be that monthly, yearly or every 3 years!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign into Discord">
    If you’re not already signed in on our website, you’ll be prompted to do so now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your billing information">
    You’ll now be sent to our checkout page, where you can enter your payment information - we use Stripe to simplify and secure our billing process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to your server">
    Once the payment has been confirmed, go to your server and type `/premium enable`
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're done!">
    You’ll now have full access to our wide range of premium features!
  </Step>
</Steps>
