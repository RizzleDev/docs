## **Overview**

If you’re reading this page, it means your account is currently **restricted from joining servers where Birbd is active**. This page will explain **why this happens**, what it means for your account, and how you can **appeal or get unbanned**.

---

## **Why You Might Be Restricted**

Birbd uses a **global moderation system** to protect servers and communities. Some common reasons for restrictions include:

1. **Violation of Terms of Service**
   - Sending spam, inappropriate content, or malicious links.
   - Harassment or abusive behavior in communities.
2. **Global Ban**
   - Your account has been banned from multiple servers where Birbd is active.
   - This is applied to prevent repeat offenses.
3. **Pending Appeal**
   - You submitted an appeal, but it is still under review.
   - Temporary restrictions remain in place until a final decision is made.
4. **System Errors or Suspicious Activity**
   - Your account may have unusual activity that triggers automatic restrictions.
   - These are temporary until your account is verified.

---

## **What You Will See on This Page**

When you check your status, the page will show:

- **Blocked Status:** Whether your account is restricted.
- **Reason:** The specific reason why you cannot join servers.
- **Appeal ID:** If you submitted an appeal, your unique identifier for reference.
- **Reviewed At:** Date when your appeal or account status was last reviewed.
- **Moderator Response:** Any message from the team regarding your ban or restriction.

<Info>
  All APPEAL ID START WITH GBR
</Info>

---

## **How to Check Your Status**

1. Enter your **Appeal Id** in the status checker.
2. You will immediately see your **current restriction status**.
3. If you are restricted, details will be displayed in clear text.

---

## **How to Get Unbanned or Unrestricted**

If your account is restricted, you can take the following steps:

1. **Submit an Appeal**
   - Go to the [Appeal Form](https://birbdbot.dev/appeal) (link to your system).
   - Provide accurate details of what happened.
   - Explain how you will **avoid future violations**.
2. **Wait for Review**
   - The moderation team will review your appeal.
   - Review times typically range from **3–7 business days**.
   - You will receive a notification once a decision is made.
3. **Follow the Guidelines**
   - If your appeal is approved, ensure you **follow community guidelines**.
   - Repeated violations may result in permanent restrictions.
4. **Next Appeal**
   - If your appeal is denied, you may submit another appeal **after 30 days**.
   - Use this time to review the rules and prepare a stronger appeal.

---

## **Important Notes**

- Appeals are reviewed **fairly and thoroughly**, but automatic restrictions may take time to update.
- If your account was restricted by mistake, submitting a detailed appeal will help the team correct it quickly.
- Do **not create multiple accounts** to bypass restrictions; this may result in permanent bans.

---
