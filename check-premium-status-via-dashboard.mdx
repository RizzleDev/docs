---
title: "Check Premium Status via Dashboard"
---

Birbd allows server owners to easily check their **premium subscription status** through the dashboard. This ensures you know whether you have access to premium features like Server Backups, Auto Moderation, and more.

---

## Accessing the Dashboard

1. Go to the Birbd Dashboard.
2. Log in with your Discord account.
3. Select your server from the list of servers you manage.

---

## Checking Premium Status

Once your server is selected:

1. Navigate to the **Subscription** section.
2. The dashboard will display:
   - ✅ **Active Premium**: Your server currently has premium features enabled.
   - ⚠️ **Inactive / Expired**: Your server does not have premium or it has expired.
   - ⏱ **Expiration Date**: When your premium subscription ends.

## Benefits of Premium Status

Servers with active premium status gain access to features such as:

- 💾 Server Backups
- 🛡 Advanced Auto Moderation
- ⚙️ Customization and Priority Support
- 📊 Dashboard analytics

---

## Tips

- 🔄 Refresh the dashboard to ensure your subscription status is up to date.
- 💳 Upgrade to premium if your server does not currently have it to unlock premium features.
- 🗂 Use this section to verify access before attempting premium-only commands or features.

Checking your premium status via the dashboard is the easiest way to confirm that your server can access Birbd's full feature set.
