Information on how to manage your Premium subscription

## Managing your plus subscription

If you bought your subscription through our website, you can manage your subscription with /premiummy-subscriptions`and`/premiummanage-subscription\`.

### If you bought your subscription through Discord

To manage your subscription, go to your user settings & then your billings page.

#### [​](https://docs.scrumpbot.com/help-center/manage-your-plus-subscription#if-you-bought-through-kofi-patreon-or-buymeacoffee)

If you bought through KoFi, Patreon or BuyMeACoffeeWe are unable to help with these subscriptions & you will need to go to the respective websites to manage your subscription - we highly encourage people to transfer their subscription to our own system, we have a guide on how to do that here.
