Information on how to transfer your Premium subscription

## Transferring a Premium subscription between servers

You can transfer your subscription once every 7 days with a maximum of 3 times per subscription - to transfer a subscription, join our [support server](https://birbdbot.dev/support) and open a help ticket!

<Check>
  This can only be done for subscriptions purchased via Stripe
</Check>
