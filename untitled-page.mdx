What is the modal timed out error & how to avoid it.

<Frame>
  ![Modal Timeout Example](/images/modal-timeout-example.png)
</Frame>

Modal timeout errors occour when if you have taken too long to click / respond to certain buttons. These errors are completely harmless & all you need to do is to re-run the command, but this time, respond a bit faster :) 

### [​](https://docs.scrumpbot.com/help-center/modal-timed-out#is-your-modal-timing-out-unreasonably-fast)

Is your modal timing out unreasonably fast? If you find that the modals are timing out wayyyy too fast (within seconds), that’s probably a bug! In that case, join our [support server](https://discord.gg/FF7mJ9z6jn) and open a help ticket!
